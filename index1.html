COVID 19

Introduction

Coronavirus disease is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.Most people infected with the virus will experience respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment.

Effects

Older people and those with underlying medical conditions like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, or cancer are more likely to develop serious illness which might be fatal too.

Protection

Protect yourself from infection by staying at least 1m apart from others, wearing a properly fitted mask, and washing your hands or using an alcohol-based rub frequently. Get vaccinated as soon as possible.
